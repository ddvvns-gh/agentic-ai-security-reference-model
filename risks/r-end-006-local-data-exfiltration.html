R-END-006

Local Data Exfiltration

Sensitive data is exfiltrated from a managed endpoint to AI services — pasted, uploaded or sent through the browser to unapproved AI.

Risk IDR-END-006
CategoryEndpoint / Data
Trust domainExecution
ReversibilityIrreversible (disclosure)
OwnerSecurity · Privacy
Parent capabilityBox 24 · Managed-Endpoint Enforcement
Description
The endpoint is where users hand data to AI. Without browser/endpoint DLP, sensitive content is pasted or uploaded to consumer AI tools, leaving the boundary irreversibly — the most common day-to-day AI data-loss path.
Threat scenario
An employee pastes a confidential customer document into a public AI chatbot for summarisation; the data leaves the enterprise boundary and may be retained or used for training.
Assessment
LikelihoodMediumPasting data into AI tools is a daily behaviour.
ImpactHighIrreversible disclosure of sensitive data.
InherentHighBefore controls.
Mitigating controls
ControlRoleEffect

C-END-006 Unapproved Local AI Tool BlockingPreventiveBlocks the unapproved AI tools used to exfiltrate.
