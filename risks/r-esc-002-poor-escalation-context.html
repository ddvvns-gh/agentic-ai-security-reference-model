R-ESC-002

Poor Escalation Context

The approver decides without adequate, accurate context — so approvals are fast but wrong, or slow and stalled.

Risk IDR-ESC-002
CategoryDecision & Accountability
Trust domainDecision
ReversibilityDecision-quality
OwnerRisk
Parent capabilityBox 39 · Pause & Escalate — High Impact
Description
An escalation gate adds little if the human approving cannot see what they are approving. Thin, inconsistent or misleading context yields rubber-stamped approvals (false confidence) or decision paralysis — both undermine the control.
Threat scenario
An approver receives a terse escalation with no risk score, reversibility or lineage. Under time pressure they approve based on trust in the agent — a rubber stamp that the gate was supposed to prevent.
Assessment
LikelihoodMediumDefault escalations are often under-contextualised.
ImpactMediumBad approvals; the gate becomes ceremonial.
InherentHighBefore controls.
