E-TCC-004

Compensation Record

The record of the compensating actions taken for an agent's irreversible effects, their outcomes, and the dependent-system reconciliation.

Evidence IDE-TCC-004
Produced byC-TCC-005
FormatStructured event + case
IntegritySigned
Retention7 yr (where financial)
ConsumersRisk · Audit
Purpose
Proves the enterprise addressed harm it could not prevent — what was reversed or offset, by whom, and whether dependent systems were reconciled. Evidence behind compensation completion (M-TCC-004).
Required fields
FieldDescription
incident_idCorrelation key.
effect_refThe irreversible effect being compensated (e.g. commitment id from Box 33).
compensating_actionRecall, correction, notification, ledger adjustment.
owner / approvalAccountable owner and approval.
reconciliationDependent-system state reconciliation outcome.
completed_within_slaWhether completed within SLA (drives M-TCC-004).
signatureTamper-evident signature.
Collection & retention

Collection

  • Produced by the compensating-action workflow / case management.
  • Linked to ledgers and the commitment register (Box 33).

Retention & disposal

  • Retain 7 years where financial/customer-affecting.
  • Immutable; disposed under records governance.
Integrity
Signed; the compensation actions and reconciliation outcome are fixed for audit and customer-remediation defence.
Assurance use
Evidences M-TCC-004 and demonstrates closure of residual harm incl. dependent-system reconciliation (backlog item 12).
