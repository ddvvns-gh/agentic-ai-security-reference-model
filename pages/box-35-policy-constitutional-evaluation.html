Assurance model

Design effectiveness

Does the design address the defined risks and control objectives?

Operational effectiveness

Is the control operating consistently across all in-scope agents and workflows?

Continuous assurance

Can evidence and exceptions be continuously monitored and reviewed?

Enterprise scenarios

Restricted disclosure

Agent proposes external disclosure.

Desired state:
Policy-as-code blocks.
Concern:
Evaluator alone says safe.

Conduct issue

Agent drafts advice.

Desired state:
Low confidence escalates.
Concern:
Ambiguous output proceeds.

Policy update

Rules change.

Desired state:
Version binding forces revalidation.
Concern:
Old evaluator used.

Regulatory and framework alignment

Regulatory relevance

  • APRA CPS 230: operational risk, resilience and accountability.
  • APRA CPS 234: control design and operating effectiveness evidence.
  • Privacy Act / OAIC APPs where personal information is involved.
  • ASIC / conduct obligations where customer outcomes are affected.

Reference frameworks

  • NIST AI RMF: Govern, Map, Measure and Manage.
  • ISO/IEC 42001: AI management system lifecycle and governance.
  • OWASP LLM / Agentic AI risks: excessive agency, unsafe output and tool misuse.
  • MITRE ATLAS: operational AI attack and response scenarios.
Future evolution

Future evolution should include policy digital twins, adversarial evaluator validation and automated policy drift detection.
