Exception Management & Waiver Governance

Ensure policy exceptions, waivers and compensating controls are formally approved, monitored, reviewed and retired — no material AI exception operates indefinitely.

Box17
Primary trust domainDecision Trust
Governance mechanismWaiver & Exception Register
StatusGold-standard draft
Purpose and strategic objectives

Purpose

Keep every exception owned, compensated, time-bound and reviewed so risk acceptance never becomes permanent or invisible.

Strategic objective

Convert the capability into a governable, measurable and assurable component of the Agentic AI Security & Governance Reference Model.

Architectural placement

Why this capability sits here

This L-1 capability is the governed escape hatch: it manages deviations from policy without letting them erode the control baseline, feeding GRC and assurance capabilities.

No material AI exception may operate indefinitely. All waivers must have accountable owners, expiry dates, compensating controls and periodic review requirements.

Enterprise scenarios

New waiver

A team needs a deviation.

Desired state:
Approved, owned and time-bound.
Concern:
Indefinite waiver.

Expiry

A waiver lapses.

Desired state:
Auto-flagged and retired.
Concern:
Runs forever.

Review

Open exceptions are reviewed.

Desired state:
Re-justified or closed.
Concern:
Never reviewed.
