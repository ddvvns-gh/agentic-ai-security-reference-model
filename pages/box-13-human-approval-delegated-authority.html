Human Approval & Delegated Authority

Ensure material decisions, commitments and threshold breaches are approved by appropriately authorised humans before execution — agents operate within a Delegated Authority Matrix with escalation and dual control.

Box13
Primary trust domainDecision Trust
Governance mechanismDelegated Authority Matrix
StatusGold-standard draft
Purpose and strategic objectives

Purpose

Bind agent action to human authority so material commitments cannot execute without the right approval.

Strategic objective

Convert the capability into a governable, measurable and assurable component of the Agentic AI Security & Governance Reference Model.

Architectural placement

Why this capability sits here

This L-1 capability is the human-authority gate in the pre-deployment and runtime path, feeding commitment, escalation and evidence capabilities.

Agents must operate within a Delegated Authority Matrix. Actions exceeding approved thresholds require escalation, human approval or dual authorisation before commitment.
