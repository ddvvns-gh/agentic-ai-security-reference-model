Yield & Evaluate – Soft

The non-blocking response path for ambiguous, low-severity semantic deviation: route the action to a deterministic policy check and constrained evaluator review, then allow or deny the specific action — without silently changing any policy boundary.

Purpose and strategic objectives

Purpose

Govern the soft response path so that ambiguous semantic deviation is evaluated and resolved at the level of the specific action, reducing both false-positive friction and false-negative risk.

Strategic objective

Convert the capability into a governable, measurable and assurable component of the Agentic AI Security & Governance Reference Model.

Architectural placement

Why this capability sits here

This L2 capability is the soft branch of the threshold routing matrix. It governs the transition from runtime behaviour to a decision, evidence and assurance outcome where the deviation is too ambiguous to hard-block but too material to blindly allow.

Soft drift creates false positives if hard-blocked but risk if blindly allowed. This capability allows or denies the specific action; policy-boundary changes require an approved policy update and redeployment.

Assurance model

Design effectiveness

Does the soft path address the defined risks and control objectives without changing policy boundaries?

Operational effectiveness

Is the deterministic-then-evaluator sequence operating consistently across all in-scope agents and workflows?

Continuous assurance

Are false-positive / false-negative trends and escalations continuously monitored and reviewed?

Enterprise scenarios

Customer-impacting recommendation

Agent proposes a decision affecting a customer outcome.

Desired state:
Policy check, evaluator review, action allowed or denied with evidence.
Concern:
Blanket block drives shadow use.

High-risk transaction

Agent requests an irreversible payment or external commitment.

Desired state:
Soft-to-hard escalation before execution.
Concern:
Treated as low-severity and allowed.

Policy exception

Team requests a temporary deviation from a guardrail.

Desired state:
Owner, expiry, compensating control, evidence.
Concern:
Soft path quietly widens the boundary.
