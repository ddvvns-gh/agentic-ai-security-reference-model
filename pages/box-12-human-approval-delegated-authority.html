Box 30 Full Depth Upgrade v3

Human Approval & Delegated Authority governs when agent autonomy must yield to accountable human decision makers.

Control Objective

Ensure material decisions, commitments and threshold breaches are approved by appropriately authorised humans before execution.

Control Statement

Agents must operate within a Delegated Authority Matrix. Actions exceeding approved thresholds require escalation, human approval or dual authorisation before commitment.

Risk Catalogue

  • R-HUM-001 Agent exceeds delegated authority
  • R-HUM-002 High-impact decision executed without approval
  • R-HUM-003 Approval fraud or inappropriate delegation
  • R-HUM-004 Authority matrix drift

Threat Events

  • Agent approves financial commitment beyond authority limit
  • Human approver lacks required delegation
  • Emergency override bypasses review process
  • Threshold configuration error enables unauthorised execution

Controls

  • C-HUM-001 Delegated Authority Registry
  • C-HUM-002 Threshold Matrix Enforcement
  • C-HUM-003 Dual Approval Control
  • C-HUM-004 Escalation & Override Governance

Metrics

  • M-HUM-001 Decisions exceeding threshold with approval = 100%
  • M-HUM-002 Unauthorised commitments = 0
  • M-HUM-003 Delegated authority review completion = 100%

Evidence

  • Delegated Authority Registry
  • Approval workflow logs
  • Override records
  • Threshold enforcement audit trail
