Enterprise scenarios

Internal draft

Agent drafts summary.

Desired state:
Low score allows continuation.
Concern:
Sensitive data missed.

Payment request

Agent requests transfer.

Desired state:
High score triggers approval.
Concern:
Score ignored.

Fragmented action

Agent splits request.

Desired state:
Pattern increases risk score.
Concern:
Fragmentation lowers score.

Regulatory and framework alignment

Regulatory relevance

  • APRA CPS 230: operational risk, resilience and accountability.
  • APRA CPS 234: control design and operating effectiveness evidence.
  • Privacy Act / OAIC APPs where personal information is involved.
  • ASIC / conduct obligations where customer outcomes are affected.

Reference frameworks

  • NIST AI RMF: Govern, Map, Measure and Manage.
  • ISO/IEC 42001: AI management system lifecycle and governance.
  • OWASP LLM / Agentic AI risks: excessive agency, unsafe output and tool misuse.
  • MITRE ATLAS: operational AI attack and response scenarios.
