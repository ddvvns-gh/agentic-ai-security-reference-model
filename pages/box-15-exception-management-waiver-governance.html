Box 30 Full Depth Upgrade v3

Exception Management & Waiver Governance ensures risk acceptance remains visible, time-bound and accountable.

Control Objective

Ensure policy exceptions, waivers and compensating controls are formally approved, monitored, reviewed and retired.

Control Statement

No material AI exception may operate indefinitely. All waivers must have accountable owners, expiry dates, compensating controls and periodic review requirements.

Risk Catalogue

  • R-EXC-001 Temporary exception becomes permanent.
  • R-EXC-002 Risk accepted without accountable owner.
  • R-EXC-003 Compensating controls are ineffective.
  • R-EXC-004 Exception portfolio exceeds risk appetite.

Threat Events

  • Expired waiver remains active.
  • High-risk control bypass operates without review.
  • Exception approval lacks appropriate authority.
  • Multiple exceptions aggregate into systemic exposure.
