Create a structured evidence trail from user intent to final outcome.

This capability defines the evidence schema for AI decisions: user request, agent identity, model, prompt, context, tools, policy decisions, approvals, output and final commitment.

Purpose and strategic objective

Purpose

To ensure AI-assisted decisions can be traced, reviewed, audited and defended.

Strategic objective

Provide the evidence backbone for security investigation, audit, regulatory review and business accountability.

Architectural placement

Why this sits here

This sits in Layer 3 because it captures cognitive governance and accountability evidence rather than raw infrastructure telemetry alone.

If you cannot reconstruct the decision path, you cannot defend the outcome.
Problem statement

AI decisions may involve multiple prompts, tools, models, retrieved documents and human approvals. Without structured lineage, evidence becomes fragmented and unreliable.

Capability description

What the capability does

  • Defines standard decision evidence schema.
  • Links prompts, context, tools, model versions and policy outcomes.
  • Records approvals, overrides and final commitments.
  • Supports search, audit and replay analysis.
  • Feeds GRC, assurance and incident response.

What the capability does not do

  • It does not expose private chain-of-thought where inappropriate.
  • It does not prove the decision was correct.
  • It does not replace raw telemetry retention.

Enterprise examples

Complaint decision

AI recommends customer response.

Normal / desired state:
Lineage captures prompt, policy, approval and final message.
Risk / control concern:
Only the final answer is retained.

Production change

Engineering agent proposes configuration update.

Normal / desired state:
Lineage links code, test, approval and deployment record.
Risk / control concern:
No one can reconstruct why the change was made.
Failure scenarios

Missing prompt evidence

Only output is stored.

Unlinked tool call

Tool action cannot be linked to user request.

Tampered record

Decision evidence can be modified after the fact.
