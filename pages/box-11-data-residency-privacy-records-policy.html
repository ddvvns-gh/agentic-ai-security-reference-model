Data Residency, Privacy & Records Policy

Enforce deterministic data boundaries, residency and sovereignty across transient and persistent AI memory — prompts, embeddings, caches, memory stores and inference endpoints all mapped to approved zones.

Purpose and strategic objectives

Purpose

Guarantee that AI data stays within approved residency, privacy and records boundaries across its full lifecycle.

Strategic objective

Convert the capability into a governable, measurable and assurable component of the Agentic AI Security & Governance Reference Model.

Architectural placement

Why this capability sits here

This L-2 capability is the data-plane control contract: it binds AI memory and inference to sovereign boundaries, feeding privacy, runtime and evidence capabilities.

All prompt blocks, vector embeddings, caches, memory stores and inference endpoints must be mapped to approved residency zones. Cross-border routing outside approved policy boundaries must be blocked.
