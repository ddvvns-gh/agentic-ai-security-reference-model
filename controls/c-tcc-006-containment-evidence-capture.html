C-TCC-006

Containment Evidence Capture

Capture tamper-evident forensic evidence of the entire containment — trigger, actions, timings and outcomes — for IR, audit and post-incident review.

Control IDC-TCC-006
TypeEvidentiary
Trust domainDecision
Enforcement pointControl plane (continuous)
Failure postureRecord-or-flag
Parent capabilityBox 34 · Terminate, Contain & Compensate — Hard
Control objective
Make every containment fully reconstructable. Without complete evidence, the enterprise cannot prove what was contained, how quickly, or whether it worked — undermining IR, audit and regulatory defence.
Control statement
Every containment must capture its trigger, the actions taken, their timings and their outcomes as tamper-evident evidence; a containment whose evidence cannot be captured must be flagged for manual record.
Design & operation
  1. Each containment control emits its action and timestamp keyed by an incident_id.
  2. Evidence is assembled into the containment record set (E-TCC-001..004) tamper-evident.
  3. Timings feed mean-time-to-containment (M-TCC-001); completeness is checked.
  4. Feeds threat detection/IR (Box 47) and post-incident review.
Inputs / outputs

Inputs

  • Per-control containment actions + timestamps
  • Shared incident_id

Outputs

  • Containment evidence set (E-TCC-001..004)
  • Timing + completeness signals
  • Feed to IR (Box 47)

Test method / assurance

Design effectiveness

  • Confirm every containment control feeds evidence on a shared key.
  • Confirm tamper-evidence and completeness checking.
  • Confirm timings are captured for MTTC.

Operating effectiveness

  • Reconstruct a drilled containment end-to-end from evidence.
  • Confirm evidence completeness for each containment.
  • Attempt tamper in test; confirm detection.
Failure modes & residual risk
  • Async gaps. Late-arriving records can momentarily appear incomplete; bounded by SLA.
  • Residual. Low with keyed, tamper-evident capture.
Regulatory mapping
  • APRA CPS 230 — incident records.
  • EU AI Act — record-keeping.
  • MITRE ATLAS / ATT&CK — IR evidence.
Implementation notes (vendor-neutral)
Implement as containment-event capture correlated via structured telemetry (Box 46) into a tamper-evident store; aligns with the decision-lineage discipline (Box 43).
