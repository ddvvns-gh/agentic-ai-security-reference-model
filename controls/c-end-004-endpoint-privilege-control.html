C-END-004

Endpoint Privilege Control

Control the privileges available to AI agents and tools on managed endpoints — least privilege, no local admin, no unrestricted local execution.

Control IDC-END-004
TypePreventive
Trust domainExecution · Identity
Enforcement pointManaged endpoint (EDR/MDM)
Failure postureFail-closed
Parent capabilityBox 24 · Managed-Endpoint Enforcement
Control objective
Constrain what AI can do on the endpoint itself. By enforcing least privilege for AI agents and tools on managed devices, a compromised or misbehaving local agent cannot escalate or act with the user's full machine privileges.
Control statement
AI agents and tools on managed endpoints must run under least privilege (no standing local admin, constrained local execution); privilege beyond policy must be denied and recorded.
Design & operation
  1. Apply least-privilege policy to AI agents/tools on managed endpoints via EDR/MDM.
  2. Deny local admin and unrestricted execution to AI processes.
  3. Enforcement events recorded (E-END-003); coverage measured (M-END-004).
  4. Couples to BYOD/contractor controls (Box 20) and device management (Box 19).
Inputs / outputs

Inputs

  • Endpoint AI process
  • Least-privilege endpoint policy

Outputs

  • Privilege-constrained AI process
  • Out-of-policy denial
  • Endpoint Enforcement Log (E-END-003)

Test method / assurance

Design effectiveness

  • Confirm AI processes run least-privilege (no standing admin).
  • Confirm out-of-policy privilege is denied.
  • Confirm enforcement covers managed AI endpoints.

Operating effectiveness

  • Attempt a privileged local AI action; confirm denial.
  • Measure endpoint enforcement coverage (M-END-004).
  • Confirm enforcement events logged.
Failure modes & residual risk
  • Unmanaged endpoint. AI on unmanaged devices evades enforcement (see Box 20 BYOD).
  • Residual. Low–Medium.
Regulatory mapping
  • APRA CPS 234 — endpoint control / least privilege.
  • ASD/CISA — limit agent powers.
  • Essential Eight — restrict admin privileges.
Implementation notes (vendor-neutral)
Enforce via EDR/MDM least-privilege policy for AI processes; integrate with managed-device (Box 19) and BYOD (Box 20) controls.
