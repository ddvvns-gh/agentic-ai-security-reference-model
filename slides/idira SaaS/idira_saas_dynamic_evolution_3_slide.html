Discussion Catalyst: How will AI change the identities we need to govern over the next three years?
Speaker notes

The discussion is not about whether identity matters. Mature enterprises already understand PAM and have invested heavily in identity controls.

The important change is the expansion of identity scope. AI, automation, cloud and machine-to-machine interactions are increasing the diversity and privilege of identities requiring governance.

Across a multi-generational enterprise estate, identity becomes a common control layer that can support consistent governance, controlled access and reduced attack paths.
